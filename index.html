About Us

Precision & Trust
Since 2008

Established in 2008, Berlin Machineries Private Limited is a leading Trader–Retailer and Manufacturer of CNC Wire Cut EDM machines and 50+ precision industrial machines. Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, we serve industries across India and export to five countries.

Under the dynamic leadership of our CEO, Mr. Sunil Dubey, we have built a reputation for quality products, transparent business practices and excellent after-sales service. With over 15 years of unwavering commitment, we have achieved more than 3000 conventional machine installations and over 1500 EDM machine installations nationwide.

"Our aim is to prioritize customer satisfaction by consistently delivering quality products. We aspire to lead the market by embracing best business practices and fostering strong relationships with our associates, clients, stakeholders and workforce."

Our 12,000 sq.ft. permanent facility at Bhairavnath Industrial Estate, Chikhali, Pune is equipped with ultra-modern machines, testing equipment and a well-trained team. We provide onsite installation, operational training and after-sales support for every machine we supply.
